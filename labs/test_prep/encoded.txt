qlpplau bhacobyoawo (tayk. 26 aywlp 1564 – 23 aywlp 1616)[a] qab am omiplbh ypaeqwlihk, yfok, ams arkfw, qlsope woiawsos ab kho iwoakobk qwlkow lm kho omiplbh pamivaio ams kho qfwps'b iwoakobk swauaklbk.[2][3][4] ho lb fnkom rappos omipams'b maklfmap yfok ams kho "taws fn axfm" (fw bluype "kho taws").[5][t] hlb ojkamk qfwcb, lmrpvslmi rfppatfwaklfmb, rfmblbk fn bfuo 39 ypaeb,[r] 154 bfmmokb, khwoo pfmi mawwaklxo yfoub, ams a noq fkhow xowbob, bfuo fn vmrowkalm avkhfwbhly. hlb ypaeb haxo toom kwambpakos lmkf oxowe uadfw plxlmi pamivaio ams awo yownfwuos ufwo fnkom kham khfbo fn ame fkhow ypaeqwlihk.[7] khoe apbf rfmklmvo kf to bkvslos ams wolmkowywokos.

bhacobyoawo qab tfwm ams walbos lm bkwaknfws-vyfm-axfm, qawqlrcbhlwo. ak kho aio fn 18, ho uawwlos ammo hakhaqae, qlkh qhfu ho has khwoo rhlpswom: bvbamma ams kqlmb haumok ams dvslkh. bfuokluo tokqoom 1585 ams 1592, ho toiam a bvrrobbnvp rawoow lm pfmsfm ab am arkfw, qwlkow, ams yawk-fqmow fn a ypaelmi rfuyame rappos kho pfws rhautowpalm'b uom, pakow cmfqm ab kho clmi'b uom. ak aio 49 (awfvms 1613), ho ayyoawb kf haxo woklwos kf bkwaknfws, qhowo ho slos khwoo eoawb pakow. noq worfwsb fn bhacobyoawo'b ywlxako plno bvwxlxo; khlb hab bkluvpakos rfmblsowatpo byorvpaklfm atfvk bvrh uakkowb ab hlb yheblrap ayyoawamro, hlb bojvaplke, hlb woplilfvb toplonb, ams qhokhow kho qfwcb akkwltvkos kf hlu qowo qwlkkom te fkhowb.[8][9][10]

bhacobyoawo ywfsvros ufbk fn hlb cmfqm qfwcb tokqoom 1589 ams 1613.[11][12][s] hlb oawpe ypaeb qowo ywluawlpe rfuoslob ams hlbkfwlob ams awo woiawsos ab bfuo fn kho tobk qfwc ywfsvros lm khobo iomwob. ho khom qwfko ualmpe kwaioslob vmklp 1608, aufmi khou haupok, wfuof ams dvplok, fkhoppf, clmi poaw, ams uartokh, app rfmblsowos kf to aufmi kho nlmobk qfwcb lm kho omiplbh pamivaio.[2][3][4] lm kho pabk yhabo fn hlb plno, ho qwfko kwailrfuoslob (apbf cmfqm ab wfuamrob) ams rfppatfwakos qlkh fkhow ypaeqwlihkb.

uame fn bhacobyoawo'b ypaeb qowo yvtplbhos lm oslklfmb fn xawelmi gvaplke ams arrvware lm hlb plnokluo. hfqoxow, lm 1623, kqf noppfq arkfwb ams nwlomsb fn bhacobyoawo'b, dfhm houlmiob ams homwe rfmsopp, yvtplbhos a ufwo sonlmlklxo kojk cmfqm ab kho nlwbk nfplf, a yfbkhvufvb rfpporkos oslklfm fn bhacobyoawo'b swauaklr qfwcb khak lmrpvsos app tvk kqf fn hlb ypaeb.[13] lkb ywonaro qab a ywobrlomk yfou te tom dfmbfm khak halpos bhacobyoawo qlkh kho mfq naufvb oylkhok: "mfk fn am aio, tvk nfw app kluo".[13]
